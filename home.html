Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

Ready to take the next step toward your future career? The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub is your one-stop shop for trusted guidance, practical tools, and the latest information to help you thrive as a student and job-seeker. Whether you are just starting your exploration or getting ready to launch your career, you will find everything you need, organized for you.

The UMSI Career Development Office is a team of career development experts devoted to providing UMSI students with strategic, customized and comprehensive career services. We do much more than help students find jobs as they near graduation. We are with you every step of the way coaching, empowering and supporting you from orientation through to commencement. We offer career coaching, career courses to prepare you for your internship search, workshops to help you develop effective job search skills, and interview preparation as well as a robust on-campus recruiting program and career fair to provide you with access to many employers who want to hire you. Everything we do is designed to help you achieve career success for a lifetime.

Why Use This Resource?

  • Everything in One Place:

    Stop searching endlessly—quickly access the resources you need for resumes, interviews, networking, mentorship, and more.

  • Student-Focused Navigation: Browse by topic or question. Key areas are highlighted so you can get moving right away.
  • Guidance Backed by Experts: All resources are recommended and reviewed by the UMSI Career Development Office, so you can trust the advice and materials you find here.
  • Always Accessible: Visit the site anytime, from any device. We’re committed to meeting the accessibility needs of all students.
  • Modern Support, UMSI Excellence: We benchmark against top peer institutions and integrate UMSI’s unique support, showcasing our commitment to your success.

What Do We Offer?

  • Career Coaching: Personalized, one-on-one support from career coaches who help you plan, problem-solve, and cheer you on every step of the way.
  • Workshops & Events: From Resume Refresh sessions to Networking 101 to Career Fairs, you’ll always find an event to grow your confidence and skills.
  • Guides & Resources: Quick-access guides on resumes, cover letters, networking, and interviewing—ready when you need them.
  • Resume Resources: Templates, rubrics, and workshops to help you build strong, polished application materials.
  • Networking Tools: Practical tips, email templates, and interview guides to help you confidently connect with professionals.
  • Job Search Support: Curated job postings, employment reports, and coaching guidance to help you plan and succeed in your search.

Career Education at UMSI

UMSI offers career courses each semester designed to help students build practical skills, gain confidence, and prepare for internships and full-time roles. These courses cover resume writing, networking, interviewing, and other professional development essentials. Below is an overview of which courses are typically offered by semester:

Course Credits Semester Notes
SI 300: Career & Internship Studio: Design Your Success 1 credit Fall Term Required for all incoming BSI students
SI 390: BSI Internship Course 1 credit Fall/ Winter/ Summer Term Beginning with Summer 2024 internships
SI 505: Career & Internship Studio: Design Your Success 1 credit Fall/ Winter Term Optional for first-year MSI students
SI 681: MSI Internship Course 1–6 credits Fall/ Winter/ Summer Term Beginning with Summer 2024 internships
SI 690: MSI Internship Course 1–6 credits Winter Term Designed to accompany MSI internships

For the latest course offerings and schedules, visit the UMSI Career Education page .

How To SUCCEED With UMSI CareerLink?

What is UMSI CareerLink?

UMSI CareerLink is your all-in-one career hub, built to connect School of Information students with the people, opportunities, and tools that matter most. CareerLink is the official UMSI career management platform (powered by 12twenty) where students can explore career opportunities, build connections, and manage their professional journey in one place. UMSI CareerLink offers on-demand access to career support: internships, jobs, career coaching, career-building resources, special events and more.

What can you do on CareerLink?

  • Discover Employers & Job Postings: Browse hundreds of internship and job opportunities, many from companies that actively recruit UMSI students.
  • Book Career Coaching Appointments: Schedule one-on-one meetings with career coaches to get personalized advice on resumes, interviews, networking, and more. Check out more
  • Target Employers & Networking: Research and connect with partner employers who collaborate with UMSI, from big tech firms to nonprofits.
  • Stay Updated on Career Events: Register for career fairs, workshops, and info sessions directly through the platform.
  • Manage Your Career Materials: Upload resumes, cover letters, and keep everything organized in one central place.
